About the Journal

The Global Science

The Global Science is a multidisciplinary open-access scientific journal. Our mission is to publish original scholarly work across diverse disciplines (IT, education, economics, social sciences, and more) through a structured editorial workflow.

Scope

  • Original research articles
  • Review articles (systematic / narrative)
  • Short communications
  • Case studies (if relevant to the topic)

This journal is Open Access. Default license: CC BY 4.0.
